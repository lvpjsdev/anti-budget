**Status:** 🔲 TODO

---

## Goal

Get to 200+ organic users through targeted Telegram channel seeding, Reddit story posts, and optimized PWA discoverability — then enable the paywall and measure conversion.

---

## Context

Distribution strategy (from DECISIONS.md and FOUNDER-OS-PLAN.md):
- **No App Store.** Zero.
- **Telegram channels** — primary virality. Personal story posts in finance channels.
- **Reddit** — SEO long-tail + community trust. Story-first, not promotional.
- **Share mechanic** — in-app virality. Every shame share is a new user acquisition.
- **Product Hunt** — credibility + English-speaking early adopters (Week 6–8 if ready).
